This course will provide you with applied knowledge of central methods in modern data science and how to use them to address neurocognitive questions correctly.

And hopefully, this course will also provide you with a strong foundation in how to conduct reproducible neurocognitive research.

In addition to welcoming you to the course, I would like to give you some helpful information and a few hints for successful completion of this course.
By now you should be very experienced with online courses.
As I see it, online courses provide a different way to study and require different skills from students.
You will find that, as an online learner, self-motivation and discipline are paramount.
This means that you are entirely responsible for establishing and maintaining a regular study system.
The course has weekly assignments that are included in the online self-paced presentation you are reading now.
This course is a flipped course, you should go over the online material and finish the assignments BEFORE the weekly online meeting.
Some of these assignments are simply designed for me to assess the level you understood the material communicated in the online content.
However, most of the assignments are a stepping stone to more complex parts to follow.
Therefore, completing all assignments is key to the successful flow of the course.
You are not only free to collaborate with your fellow students, but I also encourage you to do so.
I believe that the best way to learn something in-depth is to successfully teach it to someone else.
Having said that you will find that the course is designed in a way that assignments are personalized.
